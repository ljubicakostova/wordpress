Add NIH Cancer Dictionary Widget in the Sidebar of your Health and Medical Blog or Wesbite.

== Description ==

Add this widget to your website to let users look up terms in the NCI Dictionary of Cancer Terms. Once you’ve added the widget, there’s no technical maintenance. NCI updates to the content are automatically added to the widget application.
